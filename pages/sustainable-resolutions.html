Sustainable New Year's Resolutions for Travelers

As we welcome 2026, let's commit to exploring the world more responsibly. Here are 5 resolutions for your next German adventure.

1. Choose Trains Over Planes

Germany's rail network (Deutsche Bahn) is extensive and eco-friendly. Opt for the Deutschland-Ticket for regional travel or ICE trains for long distances to significantly reduce your carbon footprint.

2. Pack Reusables

Germany has a strong culture of sustainability. Bring a reusable water bottle, coffee cup, and shopping bag. Tap water is safe to drink almost everywhere.

3. Support Local Businesses

Eat at locally-owned restaurants ("Gaststätten") and stay in family-run guesthouses ("Pensionen"). This keeps money in the community and often provides a more authentic experience.

4. Embrace Slow Travel

Instead of rushing through five cities in a week, stay longer in one region. You'll understand the culture better and reduce travel emissions.

5. Respect Nature

When hiking in the Black Forest or Alps, stick to marked trails and pack out all trash. Leave nature exactly as you found it.
