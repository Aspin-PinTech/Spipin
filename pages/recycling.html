German Recycling Guide — Sort Like a Local

Germany takes recycling seriously. Understanding the color-coded bin system is key to fitting in and protecting the environment during your stay.

The Color-Coded Bins

Most households have 3-4 bins. Here is what goes where:

  • Yellow Bin/Sack (Gelbe Tonne/Sack): Lightweight packaging. Plastic wrappers, aluminum cans, milk cartons (Tetra Paks).
    No: Toys, toothbrushes, or glass.
  • Blue Bin (Papier): Paper, cardboard, newspapers, magazines.
    No: Dirty pizza boxes, tissues, or receipts (thermal paper).
  • Brown Bin (Biotonne): Organic waste. Fruit/veg peels, coffee grounds, eggshells.
    No: Plastic bags (even "biodegradable" ones are often banned).
  • Black/Grey Bin (Restmüll): Residual waste. Cigarette butts, diapers, vacuum bags, hygiene products.

Glass Recycling (Altglas)

Glass jars and bottles that don't have a deposit are sorted by color at public collection points.

  • Colors: White (Weiß), Brown (Braun), Green (Grün). Blue/Red glass goes with Green.
  • Important: Do not throw glass on Sundays or during quiet hours (usually 13:00-15:00 and after 20:00) to avoid noise complaints.
  • No: Drinking glasses, window panes, or ceramics (different melting points).

Deposit Return Scheme (Pfand)

Germany has a mandatory deposit system for beverage packaging. Look for the Pfand logo.

  • Einwegpfand (Single-use): 0.25€ deposit. Plastic bottles and cans with the DPG logo. Return to reverse vending machines.
  • Mehrwegpfand (Reusable): 0.08€ or 0.15€ deposit. Glass or sturdy plastic bottles. Washed and refilled.

Special Items

  • Batteries: Never in the household trash. Drop them in green collection boxes found in supermarkets (dm, Rossmann, Rewe, etc.).
  • Electronics: Take to a recycling center (Wertstoffhof) or large electronics retailers.
  • Bulky Waste (Sperrmüll): Furniture and large items require a specific pickup appointment or drop-off.

FAQs

Do I need to wash plastic containers?

They should be 'spoon clean' (löffelrein), but not perfectly scrubbed.

Where do pizza boxes go?

If greasy, they go in the Black Bin (Restmüll). If clean, Blue Bin (Paper).

Can I throw glass on Sundays?

No. Glass recycling banks have quiet hours. Avoid Sundays and holidays to respect neighbors.
