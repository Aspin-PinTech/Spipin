Germany's Green Year

In 2026, Germany celebrates its commitment to a greener future. Cities across the nation are competing for and celebrating European sustainability awards. Join us for a year of innovation, workshops, and community action.

Key Themes for 2026

  • Urban Biodiversity: Re-wilding city spaces and protecting local flora.
  • Climate Adaptation: Smart infrastructure for a changing world.
  • Sustainable Transport: The future of cycling and rail.
